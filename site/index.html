SPECEM FuseEval-lite Reproduction

A cleaned, single-GPU reproduction package for updated open-model ensemble generation. The active matrix evaluates Gemma3-12B, MiMo-7B-SFT, Qwen3.5-9B, Ministral3-8B, and a local SpecEM-4 prefused ensemble on 400 bilingual FuseEval-lite examples.

400 samples 200 English + 200 Chinese RTX 4090 sequential 4-bit runs No tokens or checkpoints included

The ensemble is a documented prefused single-GPU variant, not a hidden claim of full multi-GPU official SPECEM. It fuses four verified model outputs without reference-answer access.
